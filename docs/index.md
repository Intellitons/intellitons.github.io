# A bilingual field guide to Intellitons

Welcome to the **Intelliton Blog**. This GitHub Pages site turns the project’s research outputs
into readable popular-science essays about a bold claim: that large language models may support
stable, quasi-particle-like collective modes that can be tracked across layers, prompts, and
generation steps.

The articles are based on the code and results in the
[Intelliton repository](https://github.com/xiongzhp/Intelliton). They are written for readers who
want a rigorous but accessible explanation of what the project is measuring, why the physics
analogy is useful, and what the figures say about different models.

## How to use the site

- Use the language toggle in the top-right corner to switch every article between English and Chinese.
- Your preferred language is saved locally in the browser and reused on later visits.
- The posts below are ordered as a short four-part series, but each article can also be read on its own.

# 一份面向双语读者的 Intelliton 导读

欢迎来到 **Intelliton 博客**。这个 GitHub Pages 站点把项目中的研究结果整理成更易读的科普文章，围绕一个大胆的问题展开：大语言模型内部是否存在能够跨层、跨提示词、跨生成步骤稳定追踪的“准粒子式”集体模式。

这些文章基于 [Intelliton 仓库](https://github.com/xiongzhp/Intelliton) 中的代码与实验结果撰写，面向希望理解 AI 内部机制、但不必预先掌握高深物理或数学背景的读者。重点不是堆砌术语，而是解释项目究竟测量了什么、为什么物理视角有帮助，以及图表分别说明了什么。

## 使用方式

- 右上角的语言切换按钮可以在整站范围内切换英文与中文。
- 浏览器会在本地保存你的语言偏好，下次访问时自动沿用。
- 下面的文章按四篇系列排列，但每篇也都可以单独阅读。
