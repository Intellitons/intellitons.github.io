Welcome to the **Intelliton Blog** — a series of popular science articles exploring a bold new idea:
that large language models may harbour *quasi-particle-like collective excitation modes* that can be
tracked, catalogued, and studied much like particles in physics.

These articles are based on the code and experimental results in the
[Intelliton repository](https://github.com/xiongzhp/Intelliton).
They are written for readers who are curious about AI internals but do not need a physics or
mathematics background to follow along.
